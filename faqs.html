FAQS

Lifting weights is the primary activity of Minnesota Barbell Club. Whether it be with friends or individually the goal is to get stronger and share your progress. Weekly meetings are available to discuss personal victories, technical aspects of lifting, and the community at large. Regular events include club max out days, mock meets, and sanctioned meets as well as non lifting social events.

The University of Minnesota is one of the most physically active campuses in the nation and lifting weights is an essential part of any active healthy lifestyle. Gopher Barbell brings a competitive aspect to that. With the recent rise in popularity of strength sports like weightlifting, crossfit, and powerlifting this club fills the need for an organized community for students to participate in their hobby and have new people discover a more active lifestyle.

Members do have the opportunity to acquire membership to the USAPL, the largest federation of powerlifting or USAW for Olympic weightlifting.
