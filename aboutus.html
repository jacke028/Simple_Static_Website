About Us

Minnesota Barbell Club's primary objective is to create a community of individuals dedicated to the pursuit of strength. Every great powerlifting or weightlifting icon has said training in a group is extremely beneficial. A club team environment allows members to find others with similar strength/experienced and receive advice from those more skilled. Members are encouraged to compete in powerlifting or weightlifting competitions in local or national meets along with their teammates.

Meeting times will occur weekly. Times and locations are shared through the club social media. Members need to attend a meeting, join the Facebook group, or get in touch with and executive member in order to join.
